"Weight" aka Yarn Thickness

Confusingly for non-knitters, thickness of yarn is refered to as "weight". Even more confusing to some is the terms used to describe each thickness (weight) of yarn. While "Bulky" seems self-explanatory, what does "worsted" mean? Is "sock" thickness yarn only for socks? Can socks be made with any other thickness of yarn?

The simple answer is yes just about any item can be knit with any thickness of yarn. To keep things simple, this guide is focusing on the most common thickness of yarn used for the majority of garmets "worsted aka medium".
